What's inside

Everything you need to find your people

One hub for the places, events, groups and support services of the rainbow community — starting in Aotearoa, with the world to follow.

🗺️

A map of places, not people

Explore verified queer-friendly cafés, bars, nightlife, shops, services and healthcare across Aotearoa. Filter by category or city, or see what's near you.

🛡️

Safety you can trust

Places are ✓ Verified against current information. Community suggestions are reviewed before they appear, and every listing has a report link.

📅

Events & what's on

From drag brunches to book clubs, quiz nights to support groups — find what's happening near you and add your own community events.

👥

Community groups

Browse and join local groups — social, activism, sports, cultural — or start your own and grow it with group chats and meetups.

📚

Resources that matter

A curated directory of health, legal, housing and peer-support services for the rainbow community, including takatāpui and trans-specific support.

✨

Ask Navigator

"Kia ora! What are you looking for today?" — a friendly AI guide that helps you find places, events and resources in plain language.

Safety comes first

Built to keep our community safe

Rainbow Navigator exists to help our community find each other in real life — safely, gently, and on your own terms.

🤝

Friendship, not hookups

This is a friendship and community app. It's for finding the places and groups where genuine connections happen naturally — not a dating app, by design.

📍

Your location stays yours

We never track your live location. The map shows places, not people — "find me" only works on your device and is never stored or shared.

💜

A real code of conduct

Names, pronouns and identities are honoured here — all of them, always. No harassment, no hate, and every report is acted on.
